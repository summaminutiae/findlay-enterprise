Obituary
Floyd Haney

Floyd Robert Haney, 65, of Windsor
died at 9:30 p.m. Friday in St. John\[CloseCurlyQuote]s
Hospital in Springfield.

Funeral services were held at 10:30
a.m. Tuesday in the Shrader Funeral
Home in Arthur. Burial was in Keller
Cemetery near Lovington.

Born in Arthur, he was a son of Hugh
Emmett and Anna Haney. was a
farmer and a member of thé Ash Grove
Christian Church in Windsor. He married
Helen Van Middlesworth in 1940, and she
survives.

Other survivors include sons, Tom of
Indianapolis, Ind. and Howard of Dana
Point, Cal.; daughters, Mrs. Leland
(Bonnie) Mathias of Findlay and Jane
Haney of Altamont; brothers, Myron of
Bethany, Willard L. of Wichita, Kan. and

Hugh Jr. of Springfield; sister, Mrs.

Leonard Conradi of Grayslake; and
seven grandchildren.

One son, one brother and one sister
preceded him in death.

The family suggests memorials to the
Pine Haven Christian Children\[CloseCurlyQuote]s Ranch
in Montana.
