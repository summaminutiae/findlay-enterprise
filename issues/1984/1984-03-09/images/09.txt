Findlians in the news_

Newton, Iowa for the Iowa Flying
Farmers convention. They enjoyed a
tour of the Maytag Factory, Winpower
and a Model Railroad Museum at Colfax.
Sunday they were entertained at the
home of Marshall and Pauline Warren at
Leon. They flew home on Monday.

ANNUAL TOWN MEETING
NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN to the
legal voters, residents of the Town of
Okaw in the County of Shelby and State of
Illinois, that the Annual Town Meeting of
said Town will take place on Tuesday,
April 10th, 1984 being the second Tuesday
of said month, at the hour of 2 o\[CloseCurlyQuote]clock
P.M. at Okaw Township Building for the
transaction of the miscellaneous
business of the said town; and after a
Moderator having been elected, will
to hear and consider reports of
officers, and decide on such measures as
may, in pursuance of law, come before
the meeting.
Dated March 5th, 1964.
