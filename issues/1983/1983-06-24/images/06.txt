RESOLUTION NO. 14
WHEREAS, Illinois Revised Statutes, Chapter 48, Paragraph 39s-1, et seq., The

Prevailing Wage Law, as amended,

requires that a public body awarding any

contract for public work or doing the work by day labor shall ascertain the general

prevailing hourly rate of wages for employees engaged on such work, and
WHEREAS, the Statutes further provide that said rates of pay be publicly posted

or kept available for inspection by any interested party and a certified copy thereof
promptly filed in the office of the of State;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT AND THE SAME IS HEREBY, RESOLVED by the
Village Board of Trustees of the Village of Findlay, Shelby County, Illinois that the
determination of the prevailing wages as set forth in the list attached hereto, marked

as Exhibit \[OpenCurlyDoubleQuote]A\[CloseCurlyDoubleQuote], and which by this reference are i

ted herein, are the

incorpora

prevailing hourly rates of wages being paid to all laborers, workmen and mechanics
engaged in the construction of highways and streets in this locality and such public
buildings coming under the jurisdiction of this Village Board. |

BE IT, AND THE SAME IS HEREBY, FURTHER RESOLVED that nothing
herein shall be construed to apply to the prevailing hourly rate of wages in the
locality for employment other than public works construction as defined in the said
Act, and

BE IT, AND THE SAME IS HEREBY, FURTHER RESOLVED that the Village
Clerk for the Village of Findlay, Shelby County, Illinois, be, and he hereby is
authorized to file a certified copy of this resolution with the Secretary of State\[CloseCurlyQuote]s office

of the State of Illinois.

This resolution to be in effect on and after its passage pursuant to law.

Dated this 13th day of June, 1983.
