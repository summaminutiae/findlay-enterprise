Midway through the summer of 1982,
construction started on a new facility to
house the equipment of the Findlay Fire
Protection District. The new fire station
is located at the corner of South Second
and Dazey Streets.

Following a few minor delays in
construction the building was completed
during mid-January and will be occupied
at the end of the month.

The building has a 75 foot by 50 foot
bay area for housing the fire protection

district\[CloseCurlyQuote]s engines, ambulance and
associated equipment. A meeting room
is located in the facility as well.

The meeting room will be used by fire
and ambulance personnel and
authorized civic organizations.

The Findlay Fire Protection District
will hold an open house in the near future
to give residents in the community and
the area the opportunity to tour the new
facility.
