Entries selected for regional
town and country art show

The 18th Annual Shelby County Town
and Country Art Show was held this past
weekend at the 4-H Center in Shelbyville.
Carroll Gibbons from the Art Depart-
ment at Lake Land College judged the
adult and high school entries. Judy
Poynter of Effingham judged the kin-
dergarten through junior high entries.
Gibbons selected eleven entries from ten
exhibitors to be shown at the Regional
Town and Art Show which will
be held June 10, 11 and 12 at Kirkland

University

Campus in Decatur.

There were a total of 147 adult exhibits
shown by 46 exhibitors and 862 junior
entries by 552 junior exhibitors of kin-

Thirty-nine artists and
demonstrated their talents to over 850
guests who came to see the show. Much
thanks goes to Katie West of Cowden and
the committee of Barbara Helton, Mary
Lou Tallman, Tim Johnston, Dick Elgm,
Lisa West, Brian Barnes, Susan Hobby,

Sandy Miller and Francine Hankes.

picture entitled \[OpenCurlyQuote]\[OpenCurlyDoubleQuote]Summer\[CloseCurlyQuote]s End,\[CloseCurlyDoubleQuote]
portraying the little dog and school
books, was also selected as the most
popular exhibit overall.
