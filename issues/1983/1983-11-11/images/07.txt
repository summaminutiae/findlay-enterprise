Lucille Standley

Funeral services for Mrs. Carl (Lucille
Miller) Standley, 69, of Findlay were
held at 1:30 p.m. Saturday in the Lockart
& Sons Funeral Home in Findlay. Burial
was in the Findlay Cemetery.

She died at 5:20 a.m. Wednesday in
Hawthorne Lodge in Sullivan.

Born in Moultrie County, she was a
daughter of Oscar and May Miller. She
was a member of the Findlay Christian
Church and the Findlay Order of Eastern
Star. She married Carl Standley in 1935,
and he died in 1973.
