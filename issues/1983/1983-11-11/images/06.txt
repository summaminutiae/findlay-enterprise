ORDINANCE NO. 443
AN ORDINANCE AMENDING CERTAIN UTILITY
ORDINANCES OF THE VILLAGE OF FINDLAY,
SHELBY COUNTY, ILLINOIS
_ WHEREAS, the Village of Findlay, Shelby County, Illinois, has and does
maintain a municipal gas public utility, a municipal water public utility, and a
municipal sewer public utility for the benefit of the Citzens of the Village; and

WHEREAS, the said Village has heretofore enacted certain Ordiances
for the rates and time of payment for said utilities, namely: Ordinance No. 39% (gas
utility), Ordinance No. 394 (water utility), and Ordinance No. 416 (sewer utility); and

, it is necessary for the continued efficient use of the said utilities and
for the convenience of the citizenry of the community that the regulations adopted in
the aforesaid Ordinances be modified and amended;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT, AND THE SAME IS HEREBY, ORDAINED by the
Village Board of Trustees of the Village of Findlay, Shelby County, Illinois, as folows:
SECTION 1: That Section 2 of Ordinance No. 319 as amended by Ordinance No. 395 be
omitted, and further, that a new Section 2 be added thereto in words, phrases and
numbers as follows: (TM)

SECTION 2: a) Gas supplied to meters shall be at the following rates. The
designation M.C.F. in these rates means \[OpenCurlyDoubleQuote]1,000 cubic feet.\[CloseCurlyDoubleQuote]

For the first 500 cubic feet per month, or less: $2.94 minimum; All usage in ex-
cess of 500 cubic feet per month: $2.77 per M.C.F.

The minimum rate for each user shall be $2.94 per month, and such minimum
charge shall be collected whether gas is used or not. |

In the event that the cost of natural gas being sold to the Municipal Gas Public
Utility from the pipeline supplier shall be increased in the future by said pipeline
supplier, then the cost to consumers per M.C.F. specified above shall automatically
be increased by the same amount as the increase in price per M.C.F. being passed
from the pipeline to the utility.\[CloseCurlyDoubleQuote]
