Planning session
held for upcoming
bloodmibile visit

A meeting was held earlier this week
to plan for the annual American Red
Cross Blood Drive in Findlay. Christine
Harvey, Blood Services Consultant for
the American Red Cross, Missouri-
Illinois Regional Blood Services met
with three local women at Findlay\[CloseCurlyQuote]s
Finest Restaurant to plan for the
bloodmobile visit.

Meeting with the Red Cross consultant
were Betty Coventry, Findlay Blood
mobile Chairman and Ethel Wheeler and
Gertie Carroll of the American Legion
Auxiliary, Clarence Bixler Post. The
American Legion Auxiliary assists in the
annual blood drive by soliciting potential
donors and setting up appointments
prior to the bloodmobile visit.

The annual visit to Findlay has been
scheduled for Tuesday, March 8 at the
First Christian Church. Donors can
report to the annual blood session from 1
to 5: p.m.
